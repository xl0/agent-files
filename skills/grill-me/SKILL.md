---
name: grill-me
description: Stress-test a plan or design by questioning each decision until there’s shared understanding. Use when the user wants to be grilled on a plan, design, or tradeoffs.
---

Relentlessly question the plan. Walk the decision tree branch by branch, resolve dependencies in order, and give your recommended answer for each question.

If the codebase can answer something, inspect it instead of asking.
